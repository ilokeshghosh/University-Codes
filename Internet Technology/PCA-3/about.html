About Anweshon

The Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology abbreviated as MAKAUT is a government engineering and research institution located in Haringhata, Nadia, West Bengal, India ( formerly known as West Bengal University -WBUT), was founded on January 15, 2001. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in more than 80 specializations with a student count of over 3000 guided and instructed by over 100 academic staff and aided by the same number of administrative staff. MAKAUT is going to host the 2-day Technical and Cultural Carnival, ANWESHAN2k22 on its Haringhata campus on the 22nd and 23rd of September 2022.
